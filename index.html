Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?+

Most gyms are live within 24–48 hours. Our onboarding team imports your members, plans, and historical data for you — you don't lift a finger.

Is there a mobile app?+

Yes. Members get a polished mobile app to view plans, attendance, bookings, and renewals. Staff get a check-in app for the front desk.

Can I manage multiple branches?+

Absolutely. The Pro plan includes multi-branch management with per-branch staff roles and a roll-up dashboard across locations.

Is my data secure?+

Yes. MotionHQ runs on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit, daily backups, and role-based access control.

Do you offer support?+

Every plan includes email and chat support. Growth and Pro plans get priority support with a dedicated success manager.
